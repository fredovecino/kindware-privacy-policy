# Privacy Policy for Valor BCV

**Effective Date:** January 13, 2026  
**Developer:** Kindware Studio  

Kindware Studio ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Valor BCV mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
For a better experience while using our Service, we may collect certain information about your device. 

### Data Types Collected:
* **Device Information:** We automatically collect information about the device you use to access the Service, including the **Device Model** and **Android Version**.

## 2. Purpose of Data Collection
The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:
* **App Compatibility:** To ensure the app functions correctly on your specific hardware and software version.
* **Optimization:** To improve the performance of Valor BCV for different device profiles.
* **Internal Logging:** To monitor technical performance and troubleshoot errors.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention
The device data mentioned above is transmitted to our secure server for logging purposes. 
* **Ephemeral Nature:** This data is stored in our server logs only for a short period (typically a few days) to ensure the Service is operating correctly.
* **No Permanent Storage:** We do not maintain a permanent database of individual device identifiers or link this data to any personal identity.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Your data is not shared with, sold to, or accessible by any third-party companies.

## 5. Security
The security of your data is important to us. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We use standard protocols to protect data during transmission to our server.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
